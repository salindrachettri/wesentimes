About

Vanguards of Wesea is an initiative of the Wesean High School Students Forum, dedicated to inspiring young minds by highlighting the achievements of outstanding individuals under the age of 30, as well as child prodigies from Wesea, both past and present. Our mission is to document and share the stories of those who have made remarkable contributions to this land, shaping its future through leadership, innovation, and excellence.


We seek to feature individuals who have demonstrated outstanding leadership, resilience, and dedication across diverse fields—whether in academia, science and technology, environmental conservation, governance, social activism, sports, arts, or cultural preservation. We believe that celebrating their achievements will not only honor their contributions but also serve as a catalyst for future generations to pursue meaningful endeavors that uplift Wesea.


To maintain the highest standards of credibility, all submissions will undergo a meticulous review process. We encourage nominations from educators, scholars, professionals, peers, and community members who can provide verifiable evidence of the nominee’s impact and accomplishments. Consideration will be based on:


Demonstrated Impact: The nominee’s work should have led to tangible progress or recognition at the local, national, or international level.

Leadership and Innovation: We seek individuals who have exhibited leadership, introduced new ideas, or pioneered initiatives that contribute positively to Wesea.

Commitment to Ethical and Constructive Engagement: We will not feature individuals whose work promotes hatred, division, or violence. Our goal is to foster unity, resilience, and progress within Wesea.

Authenticity and Verifiability: Submissions must be supported by credible sources, including published articles, official recognitions, or endorsements from experts in the field.

If you know of an individual whose story deserves to be told, we invite you to submit their profile along with supporting documentation. Submissions should include the nominee’s full name, age, and area of contribution, along with a detailed summary of their achievements and the impact of their work. To ensure credibility, please provide references to verifiable sources such as news articles, official recognitions, awards, or published works. Additionally, include relevant contact details for further verification. For inquiries or to submit a nomination, please reach out to us at [whsf.enquiry@proton.me]. Together, let us celebrate and document the inspiring figures shaping the future of Wesea.


For living individuals, nominations must be submitted by a third party rather than the nominee themselves. This ensures an objective evaluation of their achievements and impact.
